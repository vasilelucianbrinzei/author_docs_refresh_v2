# Help and FAQ

## Introduction

Use this section when you need answers, escalation paths, or quick support while developing, reviewing, or maintaining a workshop.

Estimated Time: 5 minutes

## Objectives

* Know where to go for practical support
* Know which page answers which type of question
* Know when to use FAQ versus direct support
* Know when AI Developer Hub can accelerate authoring without replacing the canonical workflow

## Task 1: Open The Right Support Page

| Page | Use it for | Typical moment | Link |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Need Help? | The practical support path for workshop authors | When you are blocked and need the right contact or channel | [Open](../workshops/help-faq-support/index.html?lab=need-help) |
| LiveLabs FAQ | Common process and policy questions | When you need a fast answer before asking for help | [Open](../workshops/help-faq-support/index.html?lab=livelabs-faq) |
| LiveLabs AI Developer Hub | AI-assisted drafting, restructuring, and workflow acceleration | When you want help producing a first pass faster but still plan to validate it | [Open](../workshops/help-faq-support/index.html?lab=15-labs-livelabs-ai-developer-hub) |

## Task 2: Use Support Without Losing Momentum

1. Check the **LiveLabs FAQ** first for common questions.
2. Use **Need Help?** when you are blocked, need the right owner, or need to escalate.
3. Use **LiveLabs AI Developer Hub** when the goal is drafting, restructuring, or automating part of the author workflow faster, but verify the output against the canonical guide, preview, and validator.
4. If your question is about review timing or publishing handoffs, go back to **Validation and Publish** and use **Lab 8: Review, QA, and publishing timelines** there.
