# Start Guide

## Introduction

This section now orients authors across the redesigned guide routes that share the same workshop-authoring flow.

Estimated Time: 5 minutes

### Objectives

* Choose the right redesigned guide route before you start reading
* Use background context only when it answers a real blocker
* Jump directly into the right branch once you know the route

## Task 1: Choose The Right Guide Route

| If you need... | Open | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The shortest guided route | [Open Guided Path](../../index.html#guided) | Follow the ordered request, build, review, and publish flow. |
| A focused answer | [Open Toolkit](../../index.html#toolkit) | Jump straight to one blocker, command, tool, or QA fix. |
| The full section map | [Open Full Guide](../../index.html#guide-start-here) | Review every active section in one redesigned reference view. |
| Search across the redesign | [Open Search](../../index.html#search) | Search Guided Path, Toolkit, and Full Guide from one query surface. |
| An applied workshop example | [Open Sample Workshop Demo](../../sample-workshops/clinical-first-responder-rag/index.html) | Inspect the same design language on a full end-to-end workshop surface. |

1. Start here before you jump straight into **Core Workflow** or **Validation and Publish**.
2. Choose **Guided Path** when you want the shortest ordered journey from workshop request to publish.
3. Choose **Toolkit**, **Full Guide**, or **Search** when you already know the blocker or want the complete map before reading deeper.
